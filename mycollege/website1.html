    • Raghendra Swarup Knowledge City

    Dr. Virendra Swarup Memorial Trust Group of Institutions (VSGOI)- is situated at 'Raghendra Swarup Knowledge City'(RSKC)- a world class educational complex spread over 50 acres of lush green and beautifully landscape surroundings. Visualised as a world class center of learning by the Joint Secretary, Board of Mnagement, Dr. Alakshendra Swarup, who has been a part of various renowned institutions in India and abroad, namely London School of Economics, U.K., Lancaster University, UK & St.Stephens College, Delhi the knowledge City is a self contained township equipped with-in-house facilities to cater educational and other needs of students.

    VISION

    To be a truly world class institution of academic excellence with total commitment and dedication to quality education, research, consultancy services, training, ethics and human valuea with a holistic concern for society and environment.

    MISSION

    To nurture, develop and produce globally competent technocrats and business leaders with a strong desire to excel as professionals and improve quality of human life, society and environment.
